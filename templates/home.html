Beamma Brokers

Beamma brokers originated out of christchurch of the south island of New Zealand. the brokerage started out as a small bricks and mortar firm back in' 2016. How ever with the growing popularity of investing, and the incredible rate at which technology is taking over the world; Beamma Brokers decided to take investing online. This switch was done in the early day of 2020, and the company hasnt looked back since. In the past two years the companies market cap has nearly trippled in value, with the company reporting so great quarterly financial earnings in the quarter just gone. Beamma Brokers is founded by Joel Bremner, the current standing CEO. Joels wish is to make investing accessible to everyone.
